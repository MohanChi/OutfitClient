Instructions

1. Personalize your outfit by selecting different attributes such as clothing pieces, styles, colors, and accessories. You are encouraged to select multiple options within the graph.

2. Once you are satisfied with your selection, click the “Generate My Outfit” button to begin the creation process.

3. Wait briefly, and your customized outfit will be displayed in the dialogue box on the right side of the page.

4. To experiment with more fashion styles, simply generate a new customized outfit by making new selections within the graph.
